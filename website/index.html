OUR PROCESS

How We Work

01

Discovery & Vision Alignment

Every project begins with an intentional conversation. We discuss your core vision, target audience, brand positioning, and project timeline to establish a shared creative brief before touching a single frame.

What we discuss

  • Brand guidelines & aesthetic style
  • Core objectives & delivery schedule

Why it matters

  • Prevents unnecessary revisions
  • Ensures absolute creative alignment
02

Receiving Assets

We've made file collection completely frictionless. Send your raw footage, voice recordings, logos, brand guides, and creative references via Google Drive or WhatsApp. We inspect and catalog everything immediately.

Preferred Methods

  • Google Drive (for large files)
  • WhatsApp (for references/short assets)

Safety & Organization

  • Double-backed up storage structures
  • Strict asset confidentiality
03

Creative Direction & Mockups

If you have reference links or mood boards, we absorb them. If not, we research your competitors, study what performs, and build a customized visual tone, music selection, and typographical setup for your review.

Defined Assets

  • Pacing, rhythm & narrative layouts
  • Typography and font scales

Collaborative Direction

  • Dynamic styling references
  • Music & sound design mockups
04

Post-Production / Editing

This is where the craft lives. We build timelines from scratch, focusing on precision cuts, pacing, custom motion graphics, sound mixing, color grading, and styled subtitles. No templates are ever used.

Detailed Elements

  • Custom sound effects (SFX) layering
  • Professional color space science

AI Integrations

  • High-grade natural AI voiceovers
  • Optimized automated transcription
05

Collaborative Review

We share a secure review link for you to watch the edit on your own device. We welcome your honest feedback, note every change, and implement revisions promptly. Eligible packages include live Google Meet editing sessions.

What to expect

  • Seamless review dashboard access
  • Live-edit meetings (eligible packages)

Revisions commitment

  • Timely turnaround on all changes
  • Iterative feedback welcome
06

Final Delivery

We deliver fully polished, high-resolution final exports. Every file is optimized specifically for its destination platform (Instagram, YouTube, Broadcast) with correct color profiles, resolutions, codecs, and aspect ratios.

Formats included

  • Vertical 9:16 (Shorts/Reels)
  • Horizontal 16:9 (YouTube/Web)

Specs & Standards

  • Minimum 1080p, 4K exports
  • ProRes & H.264 high bitrates
