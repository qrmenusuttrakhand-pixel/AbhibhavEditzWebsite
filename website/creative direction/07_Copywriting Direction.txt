# COPYWRITING DIRECTION
Version 1.0

============================================================

PURPOSE

Words are the voice of Abhibhav Editz.

Every sentence should create trust.

Every paragraph should remove uncertainty.

Every headline should encourage curiosity.

Never write to impress.

Write to connect.

============================================================

BRAND PERSONALITY

The voice of Abhibhav Editz is:

Human

Confident

Calm

Friendly

Professional

Creative

Honest

Approachable

Thoughtful

Never arrogant.

Never overconflicated.

Never robotic.

============================================================

COMMUNICATION STYLE

Write like a real conversation.

Simple English.

Short sentences.

Natural flow.

No unnecessary buzzwords.

Avoid corporate language.

Avoid AI-generated sounding paragraphs.

============================================================

THE FEELING

Visitors should feel:

"I understand exactly what he does."

"I trust him."

"He understands my business."

"I would enjoy working with him."

============================================================

WRITING PRINCIPLES

Write for clarity.

Not complexity.

Explain value before features.

Solve problems before selling services.

Speak directly to the visitor.

Use "you" more than "we."

============================================================

HEADLINES

Headlines should be short.

Memorable.

Emotional.

Examples:

Create Something Worth Watching.

Stories People Remember.

Editing With Purpose.

Every Frame Matters.

Raw Footage.
Real Emotion.

Never use:

Welcome to our website.

Best Video Editing Services.

Professional Solutions.

Industry Leading.

============================================================

BODY TEXT

Body text should feel relaxed.

Easy to scan.

Easy to understand.

Maximum clarity.

Never write paragraphs just to fill space.

============================================================

CALL TO ACTION

CTAs should feel inviting.

Examples:

Let's Build Something Together

Start Your Project

View My Work

Tell Me Your Vision

Let's Talk

Watch My Process

Never use:

Submit

Click Here

Learn More

============================================================

ABOUT SECTION

Avoid writing a long biography.

Focus on why you do what you do.

Mention:

Communication.

Understanding.

Creativity.

Trust.

Problem solving.

Not achievements.

============================================================

SERVICES

Describe outcomes.

Not software.

Instead of:

Color Grading

Write:

Create a cinematic visual style that matches your brand.

Instead of:

Motion Graphics

Write:

Add movement that strengthens your story instead of distracting from it.

============================================================

PRICING

Be transparent.

Simple.

No hidden language.

Explain what clients receive.

Explain how collaboration works.

Never pressure visitors.

============================================================

PORTFOLIO

Describe each project briefly.

Focus on:

Challenge

Idea

Solution

Outcome

Avoid technical jargon.

============================================================

FAQ

Answer honestly.

Keep answers short.

Reduce uncertainty.

Remove friction.

Never sound defensive.

============================================================

CONTACT

The contact section should feel welcoming.

Examples:

Let's create something people remember.

Tell me your vision.

I'll take care of the editing.

Let's build your next story together.

============================================================

WHAT TO AVOID

Avoid:

World-class

Industry-leading

Best in the market

Guaranteed success

Revolutionary

Cutting-edge

Next-generation

AI-powered

One-stop solution

Premium quality

Creative excellence

These phrases reduce trust.

============================================================

TRUST BUILDING

Trust should come from:

Honesty

Clear communication

Simple explanations

Professional presentation

Consistency

Not exaggerated claims.

============================================================

EMOTIONAL GOAL

Every visitor should feel:

This editor understands people.

This editor understands brands.

This editor cares about quality.

This editor communicates well.

============================================================

FINAL MESSAGE

When visitors leave the website,

they should remember the person,

not the marketing.

The copy should feel like a conversation with someone who genuinely enjoys helping brands tell better stories.

Every word should build trust.

Every sentence should create confidence.

Write like Abhibhav is speaking.

Not like an advertisement.
